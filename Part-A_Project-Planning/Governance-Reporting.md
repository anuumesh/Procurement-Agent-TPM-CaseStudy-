# Program Governance and Reporting Framework

## Governance Structure

### Executive Steering Committee

Responsible for strategic oversight and funding approvals.

Members include:
- GovAI Leadership
- Department of Government Procurement
- Department of Finance

---

### Program Management Office (PMO)

Responsible for delivery coordination and execution governance.

Includes:
- Technical Program Manager  
- Product Lead  
- Architecture Lead  

---

### Delivery Squads

Cross-functional execution teams:

- AI Engineering  
- Platform Engineering  
- Data Engineering  
- Security & Compliance  

---

## Reporting Framework

### Delivery Performance Metrics

- Schedule variance  
- Sprint completion rate  
- Milestone delivery status  

---

### Quality Metrics

- AI output accuracy  
- Production defect rates  
- System uptime compliance  

---

### Business Outcome Metrics

- RFP cycle time reduction  
- Procurement lifecycle improvement  
- Annual cost savings impact  

---

## Reporting Cadence

| Report | Frequency |
-------------------------
Executive Dashboard | Weekly
Risk Register | Weekly
Budget Forecast | Monthly
Portfolio Review | Monthly
